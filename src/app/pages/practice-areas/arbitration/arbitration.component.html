Sukanya Holdings vs Jayesh H Pandya

⚖️ Supreme Court ruled that non-signatories cannot be referred to arbitration under Section 8 of the Act.

ONGC vs Saw Pipes

⚖️ Expanded the scope of public policy as a ground for setting aside arbitral awards.

DMRC Curative Petition Case

⚖️ Despite arbitration wins, Supreme Court entertained DMRC’s curative petition, signaling a shift in judicial openness.

Manipal Emergency Arbitration

⚖️ MEMG initiated arbitration against Byju’s over loan default; SIAC issued a confidential order amid complex proceedings.

Fernina Developers vs Indiabulls Housing Finance

⚖️ Delhi High Court ruled SARFAESI Act overrides arbitration for disputes post Section 13(2) notice.

Vasudev Garg vs Embassy Commercial Projects

Venue clause determined arbitration seat, overriding exclusive jurisdiction; Section 9 petition dismissed.

Dholi Spintex vs Louis Dreyfus

Despite Indian law clause, arbitration seat was London under ICA rules; Supreme Court upheld this in Yashas Wind Solutions vs GE Power.
