Dalmia Cements (Bharat) Ltd. Vs Competition Commission of India

Madras High Court dismissed the appeal filed by Dalmia Cements (Bharat) Pvt Ltd and permitted the Builders Association of India to be impleaded as ‘Intervener / Proper Party’ in the suo moto proceedings in Case no 29/2010. The case was later challenged before the Hon’ble Supreme Court regarding principles of impleading necessary and proper parties under Order 1 Rule 10 of the Code of Civil Procedure.

Micromax Informatics Ltd. v. CCI & Jaina India Pvt. Ltd. v. CCI

The impugned order directed Google to make changes to MADA and AFA agreements, potentially depriving small OEMs like Micromax of benefits. Appeals were filed under Section 53A(1) of the Competition Act before the Hon’ble Tribunal.
